Speed Trivia

Welcome to the game 'Speed Trivia'- the quiz that's simulates the familiar television game show "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?". This game will give you a good indication about your knowladge level in the subject you chose.

10 question about the subject you chose, at the difficulty level you chose.

You have 30 seconds for each question, if you dont make it next question will appear and previous will be considered as a wrong answer.

If you answer wrong 3 times - game over.
